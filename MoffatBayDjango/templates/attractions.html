Attractions

Explore Joviedsa Island and Beyond

Hiking

Discover the natural beauty of Joviedsa Island on foot. Our network of hiking trails ranges from easy nature walks to challenging summit hikes, offering stunning views of the Salish Sea and neighboring islands. Don't miss the panoramic vistas from Mount Joviedsa, the island's highest point.

Kayaking

Explore the serene waters of Moffat Bay and beyond. Our guided kayaking tours offer a unique perspective of Joviedsa Island's shoreline, hidden coves, and diverse marine life. Suitable for beginners and experienced paddlers alike.

Whale Watching

Experience the majesty of orcas in their natural habitat. Joviedsa Island is perfectly situated for whale watching excursions. From May to October, witness these magnificent creatures along with humpback whales, minke whales, and gray whales.

Scuba Diving

Explore the underwater wonders of the San Juan Islands. With its nutrient-rich waters, the area around Joviedsa Island is home to a diverse array of marine life, including colorful anemones, octopuses, and playful seals. Both beginners and experienced divers will find sites to suit their skill levels.
